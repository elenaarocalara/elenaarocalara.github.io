Hi!

Hi there, welcome to my site. My name is Elena Aroca and I'm a 3rd-year graphic design student at ESNE, living in Madrid, Spain. Ever since I was little I have always loved drawing and enjoyed every form of art that I came upon. As I grew up I became more interested in certain fields like illustration, photography and fashion among others. When the time came to decide what to do with my life I chose to study graphic design because I found really fascinating the fact that you can communicate things in a way that transcends any language barrier. Through these last three years I have been expanding my knowledge and defining my areas of expertise (illustration, editorial design, web design and programming, branding, packaging and photography). Now, I would like to hone those skills by gaining some work experience.
